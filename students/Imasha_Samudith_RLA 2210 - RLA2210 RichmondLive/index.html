RL Crew

"Richmond LIVE" , the live web casting team of Richmond College Galle for the Richmond - Mahinda Lovers' Quarrel. This Team was founded by 5 old boys of Richmond College, who were active members of Richmond College IT Society.Udara Sampath Bandara, Dilanka Madhawa Guruge, Dimuthu Kariyawasam, Yasith Samarawickrama and Thisaru Dilhara were the founders of Richmond Live in 2014 March. For the live web casting process, we are connected with the Richmond College IT Society since 2014 and 2015, Richmond College Media Unit joined with us to make this webcast better.
